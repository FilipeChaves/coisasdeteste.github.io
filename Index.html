Concept

Our Concept is to bring feelings and sounds from the recent past back to life in a very friendly atmosphere through special outdoor gatherings.
Events where energy and communication flow in a retro-futuristic way.
A unique location, under the shade of trees, in the middle of nature, built with love by dancers for dancers. All the musicians on stage are active members of the global trance scene, and have careers which started back in the 90's. We believe that the most important ingredient in a party are the participants, everyone is a part of it, everyone makes the gathering! Collective energy gives wings to a gathering, your presence makes it magical.

Chillout

Dedicated to psychill ambient and downtempo sounds. This area invites softer moments in a cosy psychedelic nest. The Chillout Area is designed and built with the aim to achieve ultimate comfort and relaxation. With consideration towards the different stages of Chill, our Chill master has developed the idea to create 3 different layers inside this stage, which are: The Chill In, The Chill Out and The Sleep Out.
The Chill In will be the area for soft, downtempo dancing.
The Chill Out will be the perfect spot to sit, have a drink, relax and listen to the music while communicating with those around you.
The Sleep Out is meant for those who feel like slumbering away comfortably in the arms of music, never losing touch with the party.
The musical program is designed to meet specific levels of relaxation at specific times of the day, always in tune with the rest of the gathering.

If you are part of a band or a solo project, feel free to submit your information in HERE.

FIRE POLICY

Fire is ABSOLUTELY forbidden in any form!!!!!!!!
Fire risk is EXTREMELY HIGH on the ZNA Gathering land, please don’t put yourself or others at risk and protect mother nature.

PET POLICY:

Be mindful: if you love your pet, don’t bring him to the ZNA Gathering.
In the region of Portugal where the ZNA Gathering is held, temperatures can rise upwards of 40ºC. This could cause extreme dehydration to your pet.
Bringing your pet to the ZNA Gathering can be a painful experience for your animal and can cause risks to public health. Think twice!!
If you decide to bring them regardless, you must be aware that the only dog friendly zone will be inside the caravan park and near it. This means it´s no allowed to bring your dog to the restaurant area or to any other recreational area.

EMERGENCY CARE:

There will be two different health care areas:
a) Emergency Green Clinic & Happy Herbal Pharmacy
b) Emergency permanent ambulance and paramedics on the site

HYDRATION:

We have free drinking water available on the site.
Our water system is fed by a underground source which is located 120 meters under the ground. The water is then pumped to feed the 15 drink fountains witch are spread throughout the venue. This water has been tested in an official laboratory. The test results say it is a pure mineral delight and completely suitable for drinking.

LAKE POLICY:

Always use the lake in a responsible way.
Make sure you’re in the right mental and physical condition to enter the water.
Do not swim down too deep or too far away from the shore.
Avoid swimming alone.
Always keep a constant eye on children as they play near or in the water.
Never use the lake as your toilet.
Do not pollute the water with soap or detergents of any kind.
Respect others and nature!!

WEATHER & PRECAUTION:

Most likely the weather will be hot and sunny so it´s very important that you bring your sun screen and never forget to drink plenty of water.

SHOWER POLICY:

The showers are supplied with the same mineral water you can find in any tap at the Gathering. Once you use that water for bathing, it is collected in tanks.
The water is then pumped and transported to water treatment facility, many kilometres away. This is a strenuous and costly task, but one we take on seriously and with great resolve.
We care for your comfort, and mother nature’s preservation, so we kindly ask you to use the shower water wisely, and make sure you use only biodegradable shampoos, soaps, etc.
Please save shower water!

ZNA History

These meetings are a consequence of the reconnection of many of the old school party people trough social media.
An online group was created in 2010 and it became clear that there were more than just a few old Zambu souls out there craving for a proper old school gathering.
Five parties have been done already under the ZNA motto, and their energy and vibration are still echoing in our hearts and souls.
Along this journey we were guided by our hearts, and supported by all our Zambu friends, both on stage and on the dancefloor.
We have had the privilege to host many of the best artists in the scene, all people we appreciate in an artistic and personal way. For us it’s about highlighting the energy and vibration off all the participants.
The result is a universal meeting point for old school trance lovers that take place in the ZNA Gathering every 2 years.
The dancers are the energy; the dancers are the key…
Our crew is a small group of trance heads who have been living and working in this scene for the last 20 years all around the world. We have been at the birth of many parties and festivals across time. It’s in our genes by now, in our ZNA…

THE INFINITY PROJECT by Graham Wood

This is one of the projects that founded the mythical label named TIP (The Infinity Project) Records back in 1994.
This project was born in the late 80's, when Raja Ram and Graham Wood joined forces creating music for all the acid heads who were thirsty for new underground futuristic psychedelic music. At the time, this was really hard thing to find...
Together with their closest friends, such as Martin Freeland (MWNN), Simon Posford (Hallucinogen) and Nick Barber (Doof) they created, for almost 10 years, flawless music under The Infinity Project. During all this time they fed the minds of many of the true Goa trance rockers of that generation, helping them to experience new sensations and to push the boundaries of reality and musical perception.
In 1998 Raja Ram and Graham Wood agreed to split up in order to concentrate their creativity on other musical projects. However, here and there, they both accepted to perform as The Infinity Project.
Each one had his own vision within the universe of TIP, and as such the 2 interpretations of The Infinity Project, grew in different directions, becoming very different from each other.

Antaro

DJ ANTARO is a wonderful artist, but first and foremost, he is an incredible human being, blessed with the most lovable character, and of course, the ZNA of a true Zambu warrior!
His personal history and work are tied together with the history of our beloved trance movement.

In 1992 he co-founded the VOOV Experience, a party that would later become Europe’s first proper Trance Festival…

For more than two decades his sets became the climax of hundreds of festivals and parties around the globe.
His absolute knowledge and supreme taste have always created a portal to magical moments of trance. His impeccable Art and technique wrap our minds and bodies in an upward spiral of energy.

LOGIC BOMB VS. TRANAN

It was the month of August in the year 2000 and our beloved Zambujal Forest was hosting one of the major moments this scene had the privilege to experience until then. During 4 nights and 5 days twelve thousand Goa heads who travelled from all corners of the world to celebrate Trance music surrounded by an overwhelming sense of freedom in the middle of an ancestral pine tree forest.

On the dawn of the 3rd day, these two young Scandinavian psy heads aka Logic Bomb got on the stage starting what many called, at the time, the best psychedelic experience ever. Why?
Because even though they started to produce music in the mid-nineties when Goa music was at its peak, full of layers and melodies, they gave a very unique input to the scene and started to create something different from everyone else. They were not yet known by many people, since they had just edited the first couple of tracks.

So by adding this new musical approach which was rising from some music studios around Europe they started to create unique cutting edge psychedelic music with a lot of new production skills and sound quality which became a break-through moment for the trance scene. From that precise day, Logic Bomb became a huge reference to all the trance community, and responsible for creating many dancefloor anthems that are played all over the world by all the major DJs.
For the next ZNA edition Jonas Perttersoon accepted the challenge to deliver a very unique performance for the groovy afternoon hours where his Logic Bomb universe will meet his Tranan soundscapes in order to give us two full hours of proper Psychedelic Pollution which will lead us in to Paradise.
