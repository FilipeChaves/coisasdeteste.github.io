Concept

Our Concept is to bring feelings and sounds from the recent past back to life in a very friendly atmosphere through special outdoor gatherings.
Events where energy and communication flow in a retro-futuristic way.
A unique location, under the shade of trees, in the middle of nature, built with love by dancers for dancers. All the musicians on stage are active members of the global trance scene, and have careers which started back in the 90's. We believe that the most important ingredient in a party are the participants, everyone is a part of it, everyone makes the gathering! Collective energy gives wings to a gathering, your presence makes it magical.

Beat Bizarre

Martin comes directly from the headquarters of Iboga records and is considered as one of the pioneers and one of the finest Danish musicians within this style.
Martin aka Beat Bizarre, turned the paradigm of Psychedelic trance upside down in the end of the 90´s by releasing his 1st vinyl which "instead of using the ecstatic melodies of classic Trance productions, its hypnotic power evolves from booming basses and tight percussion, from subliminal rhythmic build-ups, and from a multitude of grinding and jangling sounds that merge into a perfectly sleek groove".
Even though he has been exploring different musical approaches, at the ZNA he will deliver a special 2 hours live set that will take us apart and reunite us again in psychedelic glory.
All of us will be invited to re-set our minds in order to explore the realms of psychedelia which await us all during the hours of the night.

Miranda

Miranda is a female producer who is dubbed by many as the Queen of trance. Since the early 90´s Miranda earned the title of the very 1st solo female GoaTrance producer. The feminine energy is an extremely energetic and positive one, characterized by its creativity and power.
It is not easy to find a female musician who is also a computer nerd, who chooses to dedicate her time combining the two. She did so, and the result helped to implant the roots of our trance history.
We believe that her sensitivity combined with her vision of a proper psychedelic experience through digital music were the reason for the birth of many of the most uplifting tracks to ever hit the dancefloors.
Actually we know for a fact that when she is in the perfect mood and playing at the right time for the right crowd. She has the rare ability to provoke a euphoric state on a dancefloor, creating one of those special moments of oneness we all aim to experience time after time.
We all know she likes to explore all genres inside the psy trance spectrum. However, at the ZNA she will greet us with her majestic uplifting side, showing us once again all her beauty and power.

Chillout

Dedicated to psychill ambient and downtempo sounds. This area invites softer moments in a cosy psychedelic nest. The Area is designed and built with the aim to achieve ultimate comfort and relaxation. With consideration towards the different stages of Chill, our Chill master has developed the idea to create 3 different layers inside this stage, which are: The Chill In. The Chill Out and The Sleep Out. The Chill In will be the area for soft, downtempo dancing. The Chill Out will be the perfect spot to sit, have a drink, relax and listen to the music while communicating with those around you. The Sleep Out is meant for those who feel like slumbering away comfortably in the arms of music, never losing touch with the party. The musical program is designed to meet specific levels of relaxation at specific times of the day, always in tune with the rest of the gathering.

Goa Guardians Night

This is the day you settle in, build your nest and recover from your journey here. During this “landing” day, we will all meet at the chill stage, to get the party started through the musical hands of the most fantastic Goa Guardians! These are very special Djs form different corners of the psy world. People who are responsible for collecting, cataloguing, publishing and preserving our Trance music heritage. True guardians, dedicated to keeping the old school Goa tradition alive. Some of them have fantastic musical collections with versions of tracks witch are so rare, that even the people who produced them don’t have them anymore. Make no mistake, this will not be a chill out session, it will be a full power dance night! This will be the perfect moment to dance, reconnect with old friends and meet new ones. The energy of all of you will guide us all into a ZNA connection. And this moment will get the party started… Friendship will welcome us into the arms of our magical Gathering as we prepare for an unprecedented psychedelic journey!

FIRE POLICY

Fire is ABSOLUTELY forbidden in any form!!!!!!!!
Fire risk is EXTREMELY HIGH on the ZNA Gathering land, please don’t put yourself or others at risk and protect mother nature.

PET POLICY:

Be mindful: if you love your pet, don’t bring him to the ZNA Gathering.
In the region of Portugal where the ZNA Gathering is held, temperatures can rise upwards of 40ºC. This could cause extreme dehydration to your pet.
Bringing your pet to the ZNA Gathering can be a painful experience for your animal and can cause risks to public health. Think twice!!
If you decide to bring them regardless, you must be aware that the only dog friendly zone will be inside the caravan park and near it. This means it´s no allowed to bring your dog to the restaurant area or to any other recreational area.

EMERGENCY CARE:

There will be two different health care areas:
a) Emergency Green Clinic & Happy Herbal Pharmacy
b) Emergency permanent ambulance and paramedics on the site

HYDRATION:

We have free drinking water available on the site.
Our water system is fed by a underground source which is located 120 meters under the ground. The water is then pumped to feed the 15 drink fountains witch are spread throughout the venue. This water has been tested in an official laboratory. The test results say it is a pure mineral delight and completely suitable for drinking.

LAKE POLICY:

Always use the lake in a responsible way.
Make sure you’re in the right mental and physical condition to enter the water.
Do not swim down too deep or too far away from the shore.
Avoid swimming alone.
Always keep a constant eye on children as they play near or in the water.
Never use the lake as your toilet.
Do not pollute the water with soap or detergents of any kind.
Respect others and nature!!

WEATHER & PRECAUTION:

Most likely the weather will be hot and sunny so it´s very important that you bring your sun screen and never forget to drink plenty of water.

Parking & Camping

We have chosen the shadiest area of the ZNA Land to provide comfort for all Zambu campers.
You are welcome to bring your tent and use this area to build your nest.
Do not ever forget it is ABSOLUTELY FORBIDDEN TO USE FIRE in any form, anywhere on the ZNA Land.
Empower your green consciousness by using the multiple garbage bins around the land.

This area will have toilets and showers. Make sure you only bring biodegradable cosmetics (shampoo, soap etc)

SHOWER POLICY:

The showers are supplied with the same mineral water you can find in any tap at the Gathering. Once you use that water for bathing, it is collected in tanks.
The water is then pumped and transported to water treatment facility, many kilometres away. This is a strenuous and costly task, but one we take on seriously and with great resolve.
We care for your comfort, and mother nature’s preservation, so we kindly ask you to use the shower water wisely, and make sure you use only biodegradable shampoos, soaps, etc.
Please save shower water!
Be responsible!

ZNA History

These meetings are a consequence of the reconnection of many of the old school party people trough social media.
An online group was created in 2010 and it became clear that there were more than just a few old Zambu souls out there craving for a proper old school gathering.
Five parties have been done already under the ZNA motto, and their energy and vibration are still echoing in our hearts and souls.
Along this journey we were guided by our hearts, and supported by all our Zambu friends, both on stage and on the dancefloor.
We have had the privilege to host many of the best artists in the scene, all people we appreciate in an artistic and personal way. For us it’s about highlighting the energy and vibration off all the participants.
The result is a universal meeting point for old school trance lovers that take place in the ZNA Gathering every 2 years.
The dancers are the energy; the dancers are the key…
Our crew is a small group of trance heads who have been living and working in this scene for the last 20 years all around the world. We have been at the birth of many parties and festivals across time. It’s in our genes by now, in our ZNA…
