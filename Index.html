Concept

Our Concept is to bring feelings and sounds from the recent past back to life in a very friendly atmosphere through special outdoor gatherings.
Events where energy and communication flow in a retro-futuristic way.
A unique location, under the shade of trees, in the middle of nature, built with love by dancers for dancers. All the musicians on stage are active members of the global trance scene, and have careers which started back in the 90's. We believe that the most important ingredient in a party are the participants, everyone is a part of it, everyone makes the gathering! Collective energy gives wings to a gathering, your presence makes it magical.

Chillout

Dedicated to psychill ambient and downtempo sounds. This area invites softer moments in a cosy psychedelic nest. The Area is designed and built with the aim to achieve ultimate comfort and relaxation. With consideration towards the different stages of Chill, our Chill master has developed the idea to create 3 different layers inside this stage, which are: The Chill In. The Chill Out and The Sleep Out. The Chill In will be the area for soft, downtempo dancing. The Chill Out will be the perfect spot to sit, have a drink, relax and listen to the music while communicating with those around you. The Sleep Out is meant for those who feel like slumbering away comfortably in the arms of music, never losing touch with the party. The musical program is designed to meet specific levels of relaxation at specific times of the day, always in tune with the rest of the gathering.

Goa Guardians Night

This is the day you settle in, build your nest and recover from your journey here. During this “landing” day, we will all meet at the chill stage, to get the party started through the musical hands of the most fantastic Goa Guardians! These are very special Djs form different corners of the psy world. People who are responsible for collecting, cataloguing, publishing and preserving our Trance music heritage. True guardians, dedicated to keeping the old school Goa tradition alive. Some of them have fantastic musical collections with versions of tracks witch are so rare, that even the people who produced them don’t have them anymore. Make no mistake, this will not be a chill out session, it will be a full power dance night! This will be the perfect moment to dance, reconnect with old friends and meet new ones. The energy of all of you will guide us all into a ZNA connection. And this moment will get the party started… Friendship will welcome us into the arms of our magical Gathering as we prepare for an unprecedented psychedelic journey!

Zambukids

We are aware that many of our party rockers are in a moment on their journey where they have this wonderful and unique gift called family. And as your family is our family we want them to feel safe, comfortable and happy to be in our Gathering. As such, we developed an area dedicated to the young ones. Here you can choose to participate in activities together with your children for no cost, or give the honour to our fully trained childcare professionals of taking care of your kid(s) while you experience a moment for yourself. We guarantee a lot of fun and joy for everyone involved. The team for this area was selected for their dedication, love and last but not least - professionalism and experience, in order to give your family an impeccable service. We advise everyone who wishes to book childcare services, and get more info on prices and conditions, to please send an email to zambukids@znagathering.com

Kids are the future of our ZNA!!
